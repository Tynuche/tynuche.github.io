About us

NKZ Investments Group (Pty) Ltd was established and registered in 2019 in terms of section 14 of the companies Act 2008 and we are fully compliant with the relevant trade licensees of South African revenue authority, national mechanical engineering authority and certified under the disadvantaged group (youth development, women and people living with disabilities).

NKZ Investments Group (Pty) Ltd offers a wide range of services from electrical, mechanical engineering services and supplies, labour hire. We also do plant hire, general and industrial cleaning services, office supplies, mining supplies and services and including abnormal transportation of earthmoving and construction machinery.
